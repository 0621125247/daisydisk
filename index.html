DaisyDisk gives you a perfect overview of all volumes currently mounted, be it internal disk, USB thumbstick, FireWire drive, network or even virtual disk mounted with FUSE. Mount disk images, attach external drives and they're immediately displayedin DaisyDisk's list. Add, delete files, unmount disks... all the changes are real time, so you always know which volumes are mounted and how much free space you have on each of them. You can also get volume info and eject volumes from within the application.
